About Us

Welcome to
Ambady Estate

Ambady Estate is a serene luxury boutique resort set on the periphery of a 70-acre cardamom plantation. Designed for travellers seeking peace, privacy and authentic Munnar charms, our property blends colonial heritage with modern comfort.

Built around nature, Ambady Estate offers standalone cottages and a private villa, plantation trails, scenic viewpoints, traditional spice experiences, unique locovore cuisine and warm Kerala hospitality. Our USP is the traditional curing house, our adjoining pure certified organic farm, and a goshala housing rare breeds of indigenous cows.
